Soccer is the most impactful sport in the World

Soccer, which is more commonly known as futball around the World, is the most famous sport. All around the World people know and love soccer. Soccer is a very accessible sport to play because to play soccer it is almost irrelevant how poor the region is. It is known that the ball and goalposts can be made from a variety of accessible materials put together. For example, a group of boys in the middle school during a break made a ball only using paper and made goal posts by placing four backpacks in a corridor to play soccer. Majority of soccer rules are very simplistic and intuitive for variety of cultures and ethnicities to understand. The basic soccer game looks like two groups of players trying to get the ball in between two goalposts only using their legs. So many people around the World know and play and love soccer that they find this as a unifying practice between people that are completely different. Through soccer, people achieve respect, understanding, compassion, and more; so the impact of soccer on global level cannot be fully measured.
